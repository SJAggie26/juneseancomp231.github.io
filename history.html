Our History

Learn more about how AggieServe has impacted the community over the years.

Learn With D9

Date: 10th June, 2023

The great members of the Divine 9 hosted a summer tutoring session for our Aggie freshmen. Over 100 students attended, with 80 D9 members in attendance to volunteer. The orgs also gave away one $2,000 scholarship to a student that attended the event.

Aggie Camp 24

15th May 2024

200 NCAT Aggies held an impactful summer camp, offering a dynamic experience for students across various fields. The camp provided hands-on learning in STEM, business, and the arts, helping students enhance their skills and explore future career paths

Blue and Gold Hurricane Relief

30th September 2024

The NCAT Honors Program and the Blue & Gold Marching Machine band joined forces to provide crucial support to communities affected by Hurricane Helene. Together, they organized a campus-wide donation drive, collecting essential supplies like food, water, clothing, and hygiene products. Their collaborative effort resulted in the collection of over 1,000 items.

Field Day '22

Spring '22

The NCAT Aggies Football team recently hosted an exciting field day at a local middle school, bringing energy and enthusiasm to the young students. Team members engaged the kids in a variety of fun activities, including drills, games, and team-building exercises, promoting teamwork and sportsmanship.

GHOE Cleanup

15th November '23.

After a restless GHOE, 75 Aggies came together to cleanup around campus.

NCAT UPD Campus Walk

1st September '24

The NCAT University Police Department (UPD) recently conducted a safety walk around campus as part of their ongoing safety initiative. This proactive event aimed to assess and enhance campus security
